Designing for Field Researchers

Helping an AI qualitative research platform onboard complex clients across India and beyond.

RoleService Designer
CompanyDots by Ooloi Labs
Duration6 Months

Add: MAD Storytelling Initiative hero image

The Context

AI tools for people doing real work in the field

Dots by Ooloi Labs builds an AI-powered qualitative research platform for NGOs, social scientists, and field researchers working on public health, education, and gender equity across the Global South.

Add: Dots platform context image

The Challenge

Users who don't fit the SaaS mold

  • NGOs with hundreds of field volunteers
  • Research institutions running longitudinal studies
  • Low-bandwidth, resource-constrained environments
  • Varying tech literacy across teams
not your typical user personas
Add: Service blueprint / journey map

My Role

End-to-end ownership

I owned the MAD Storytelling Initiative from first discovery call to a live platform. Not just interfaces. The entire service.

  • Stakeholder research with NGO leaders
  • Full service blueprinting
  • Onboarding flow design
  • Research ops frameworks
  • Product-market fit with founders

The Approach

Immerse, map, intervene

I embedded myself in the context. Discovery calls with MAD's team, mapping how volunteers collected stories in the field, identifying what broke down, and finding where Dots could intervene.

"The problem wasn't the technology. It was the gap between how the platform was designed and how field researchers actually worked."
