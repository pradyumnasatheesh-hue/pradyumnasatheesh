About Me

I ask why
before I ask how

Most designers skip straight to wireframes. I start in the field — talking to real people, mapping real systems, finding the invisible forces that make or break a product before a single pixel is placed.

I came up through industry first — shipping real products at Dots by Ooloi Labs. I know exactly what kills a great idea before launch, and how to stop it.

Now pursuing my M.S. in HCI at Indiana University Bloomington, combining industry rigor with deep research methodology. Evidence-backed and execution-ready.

My Process

Research-led.
Execution-ready.

01
Discover

I start where users actually are — in the field, in context. Ethnography. Contextual inquiry. Watching what happens instead of asking what they think happens.

02
Synthesize

Raw data is noise. I translate fieldwork into insight — finding patterns that change the brief, and tensions no stakeholder knew existed until I mapped them.

03
Design

From insight to interaction. Systems that hold up under real pressure — not just in Figma, but in the hands of actual people with actual problems.

04
Validate

Assumptions are expensive. Test early, iterate fast, build the evidence base that gives teams confidence to ship — and stakeholders confidence to fund.
