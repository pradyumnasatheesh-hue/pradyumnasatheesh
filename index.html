About

I ask why before
I ask how

I came up through product design in industry, which means I don't just study interfaces, I've shipped them. At Dots by Ooloi Labs, building AI research tools for social impact organisations across India, I learned that the real design problem is almost never the one in the brief.

Now at Indiana University Bloomington, I'm studying how people negotiate agency within complex sociotechnical systems. My practice lives at the intersection of HCI research, service design, and the messy, human economics that sit underneath every screen.

I believe good design starts in the field, gets sharper in the strategy room, and only earns its name when it ships.
